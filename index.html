Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Bohan Zhuang. My research interests lie in the fields of image/video generation. During my Ph.D. studies, I had a wonderful time interning at Kuaishou MMU, Microsoft, Taobao and Tmall Group, Tecent WeChat, JD Explore Academy.
